1967 Chevrolet Impala

Vision

We’re Changing the Way the World Thinks About Chevy Impala

The 1967 Impala came with a standard 250 cubic inch Turbo Thrift 6 (that made 155 horsepower). The 427 cubic inch Turbojet V8 (425 hp) was the top engine choice available. Most engine options in the 1967 Chevy Impala were carryovers from the previous year. One of the most popular models, the SS Convertible came studded with a 327 cubic inch V8 small block engine, also known as Turbo Fire 327 that made about 275 horsepower and 355 lb-ft of torque.
